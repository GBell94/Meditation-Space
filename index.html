Who are we?

We are independent teachers of transcendental meditation, known as TM. We have courses for individuals and groups, online and in-person.

What is TM?

TM is a simple, accessible and effortless technique which is easy to learn. It involves the use of a silent mantra (word or sound). It is practised for a maximum of 40 minutes each day, usually in two 20 minute sessions, while sitting comfortably with eyes closed. You can practice at home or even while travelling by bus or train! Unlike other techniques, there is no need to concentrate or empty your mind.

What are the benefits?

There are an increasing number of scientific studies which show the benefits of regular practice, including:

  • Deep relaxation and improved sleep
  • Reduced anxiety
  • Decreased blood pressure
  • Reduced stress
  • Improved overall brain function
